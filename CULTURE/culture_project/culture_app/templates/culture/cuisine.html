Traditional Indian Cuisine

Biryani

Biryani is a flavorful rice dish made with aromatic spices, herbs, and marinated meat or vegetables, popular across India with regional variations.

Masala Dosa

Masala Dosa is a famous South Indian dish consisting of a crispy rice crepe filled with spicy potato masala, served with coconut chutney and sambar.

Butter Chicken

Butter Chicken is a rich, creamy, and mildly spiced curry made with marinated chicken cooked in a tomato-based gravy, known for its velvety texture.

Gujarati Thali

Gujarati Thali offers a variety of vegetarian dishes, including curries, dals, sweets, and pickles, served together on a single platter.

Rogan Josh

Rogan Josh is a fragrant and flavorful Kashmiri curry made with tender pieces of lamb, cooked in a rich sauce of yogurt, garlic, and aromatic spices.

Jalebi

Jalebi is a popular Indian dessert made of deep-fried, crispy spirals soaked in sugar syrup, enjoyed for its sweet and tangy taste.

Idli Sambhar

Idli Sambar is a popular South Indian dish that is widely loved across India and even internationally. It consists of two main components: Idli and Sambar, often served with chutneys.

Dal Chawal

Dal Chawal is a simple, comforting, and nutritious Indian meal consisting of dal (lentil curry) and chawal (steamed rice).
