Traditional Art & Craft of India

Madhubani Painting

Madhubani is a traditional folk art form from Bihar, characterized by intricate patterns and vibrant colors depicting nature, mythology, and folklore.

Warli Art

Warli art, originating from Maharashtra, uses simple geometric shapes to depict the daily life of the Warli tribe, celebrated for its simplicity and storytelling.

Pattachitra

Pattachitra is a traditional painting style from Odisha and West Bengal, known for its intricate details, mythological themes, and vibrant colors.

Terracotta Pottery

Terracotta pottery is one of the oldest crafts of India, showcasing the skilled use of natural clay to create beautiful and functional pieces.

Wood Carving

Wood carving is an ancient craft of India, producing intricately designed furniture, sculptures, and decor items that reflect the artistry of different regions.

Tanjore Painting

Tanjore Painting is a classical South Indian art form that originated in the town of Thanjavur (Tamil Nadu) during the 16th century under the rule of the Nayakas and Marathas.

Miniature Painting

Miniature Painting is a highly detailed, small-scale art form that originated in India during the medieval period, flourishing under the Mughal Empire and later in Rajput, Pahari, and Deccan schools of art.
